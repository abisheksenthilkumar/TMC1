ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

At SRM University, the Mathematics Department takes center stage with its captivating focus on numbers and mathematical equations. It plays a pivotal role in nurturing the intellectual growth of students pursuing various academic paths, including B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Ph.D. The department's educational scope extends its reach to encompass engineering disciplines, offering students a comprehensive mathematical foundation for their studies. Through its dedicated faculty and innovative teaching methods, SRM's Mathematics Department equips students with the essential mathematical skills and knowledge needed to excel in their chosen fields of study. It is a hub of intellectual exploration and discovery for individuals from diverse academic backgrounds.



ABOUT THE CLUB

In 2023, The Matrix Club, formerly known as the Elite Mathematics Club, continues to flourish as a dynamic and inclusive platform for mathematics enthusiasts. Established in 2011 with the primary goal of encouraging active student participation in various mathematical events, The Matrix Club has evolved into a vibrant community that fosters a deep appreciation for mathematics.

The club remains committed to nurturing budding mathematicians, offering them a unique platform to excel in the field. Its core mission is to make mathematics an engaging and enjoyable subject, striving to instill a love for the subject in all its members.

Notably, The Matrix Club conducted its inaugural National level Maths Symposium, ENIGMA'11, on March 18, 2011, featuring esteemed chief guests, Dr. G. Ravindran and Mr. S.K. Dogra, who shared invaluable life lessons and inspiration.

In the present day, the club continues to host a diverse range of events and activities. It frequently organizes guest lectures, workshops, and quiz competitions to engage students and enhance their mathematical skills. These activities not only develop their mathematical prowess but also stimulate their intellectual curiosity.
